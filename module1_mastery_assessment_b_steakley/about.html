About

Pookie Pomodoro is a fun interactive site where you can fully customize your productivity, with help from your personalized character. Our oustanding features include to do lists, routines, reminders, and a pomodoro timer all while focusing on making your every-day daunting tasks a little less scary and more enjoyable.
